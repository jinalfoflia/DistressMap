Untold Stories

Anonymously share your story of abuse, harassment or any incident that you want the world to know.

A project by Jinal Foflia.
Read more about why this was created.

Click on the map and add your story. Your story could have the following details

  • - An approximate location of the place where the incident took place is ideal. This will be helpful for others as it serves a warning. Do not name any house, business, etc
  • - Write your story and click SAVE to share it with the world!
  • - These stories will be verified by the admin before going live on the map
  • - Once you share your story, it cannot be deleted, until requested for
  • - This map becomes one place where these stories can be shared and maybe your story can help somone or give an assurance that they are not alone
  • - You do not reveal any personal details of the story (like the name of the attacker, etc)
  • - Any suggestions and Feedback can be shared here
